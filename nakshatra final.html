About Us

Nakshatra is the official Astronomy & Space Society of NSUT. We are a group of space enthusiasts who believe the night sky is not just to be admired, but explored. From stargazing nights to rocket-building workshops, we aim to bring the wonders of the cosmos closer to everyone.
